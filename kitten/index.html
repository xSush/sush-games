Kitten Clicker


Welcome to Kitten Clicker! Kitten Clicker is an Idle game made by CatR3kd, where your goal is to ascend form a fish monopoly and go down in hiss-tory for you and your cat friends :3
Below is a list of all the available upgrades, good luck!



More Fish

More fish is pretty simple: Once you buy it, you get 1 more fish every time you click!


Fisherman

For every fisherman you have, you get 1 fish per second!


Fisherman Recruiter

Fisherman recruiter will buy one fisherman every second, (If possible) without raising the price. Only one is unlockable.


Cybernetic Fisherman

Just like a fisherman, except instead of 1 fish per second, it's 100


Kitten Sidekick

Kitten Sidekicks will click once a second, but instead of giving you 1 fish, it gives you the amount of fish you earn from clicking!


Kitten Overlord

Similar to the fisherman recruiter, but for kitten sidekicks. Only one is unlockable.
